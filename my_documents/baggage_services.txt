Baggage Policies and Services

Carry-on Baggage:
- Personal item: Free (purse, laptop bag, small backpack)
- Standard carry-on: Free for Main Cabin and above, $60 for Basic Economy
- Maximum dimensions: 22" x 14" x 9" (56cm x 35cm x 23cm)
- Weight limit: 40 lbs (18 kg)

Checked Baggage Fees:
- First bag: $35 domestic, $60 international
- Second bag: $45 domestic, $100 international
- Oversized bags (62-80 inches): Additional $100
- Overweight bags (51-70 lbs): Additional $100

Special Items:
- Sports equipment: $150 each way
- Musical instruments: Varies by size
- Pet carriers: $125 in-cabin, $200 checked

Baggage Services:
- Lost baggage claims: Report immediately at baggage service office
- Delayed baggage: We'll deliver to your address free of charge
- Damaged baggage: Take photos and report before leaving the airport

Frequent flyer elite members and premium cabin passengers receive complimentary checked bags based on their status level.

For fragile items, consider our special handling service for an additional fee to ensure extra care during transport.
