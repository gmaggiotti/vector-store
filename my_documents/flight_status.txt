Flight Status Inquiry

How to check your flight status:

1. Visit our website or mobile app
2. Enter your confirmation number or ticket number
3. Provide your last name as it appears on the booking
4. View real-time flight information including:
   - Departure and arrival times
   - Gate information
   - Terminal details
   - Delays or cancellations
   - Baggage claim carousel

You can also call our customer service line at 1-800-FLY-HERE for flight status updates. Our automated system is available 24/7, and live agents are available from 6 AM to 11 PM local time.

For frequent travelers, sign up for flight alerts via SMS or email to receive automatic notifications about any changes to your flight schedule.

If your flight is delayed by more than 2 hours, you may be eligible for meal vouchers or hotel accommodations depending on the circumstances.
